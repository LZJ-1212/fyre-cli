About the Genshin Impact Wiki

Your comprehensive database for all characters in the world of Teyvat.

Our Purpose

The Genshin Impact Wiki is a fan-driven project dedicated to providing accurate, up-to-date information about every playable character in Genshin Impact. Whether you're a new Traveler just starting your journey or a seasoned veteran looking for specific stats, our goal is to be your go-to resource for character details.

We believe in the power of community knowledge. This wiki is built by fans, for fans, and we strive to maintain the highest standards of accuracy and completeness. Every character entry includes their element, weapon type, region, and a detailed description to help you understand their lore and gameplay role.

Data Sources

All character data is sourced from official Genshin Impact game files and verified against in-game information. We cross-reference multiple reliable sources to ensure accuracy:

  • Official Genshin Impact game data (version 4.x)
  • Hoyoverse official website and patch notes
  • Community-verified databases and wikis
  • In-game character screens and story quests

Character images are fetched dynamically from the Genshin Impact API and official sources. We update our database regularly to include new characters as they are released.

Technology Stack

This wiki is built with modern web technologies to provide a fast, responsive experience:

Frontend

  • HTML5 & Tailwind CSS
  • Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Responsive design

Backend
