About This Wiki

Your ultimate community-driven resource for everything Teyvat — characters, lore, and more.

Our Mission

We aim to provide accurate, up-to-date information about every character in Genshin Impact. From the Traveler to the latest 5-star releases, our wiki is built by fans, for fans. Every detail — elements, weapons, lore — is curated to help you build the perfect team.

Key Features

  • ✓ Character Gallery — Browse all characters with filters by element.
  • ✓ Live Data — Information fetched directly from our database.
  • ✓ Responsive Design — Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • ✓ Community Driven — Contributions welcome via GitHub.
